WebDAV Drive Support

Mount library as a local network drive

How to Connect

Quick Command
net use Z: "{{ activeWebdavUrl }}"
  • Open File Explorer and click "This PC".
  • In the top menu, click "Map network drive".
  • Paste the URL into the "Folder" field.
  • Click Finish. If prompted, enter any username and your PIN.
Quick Command
osascript -e 'mount volume "{{ activeWebdavUrl }}"'
  • Open Finder and press Cmd + K.
  • Paste the URL into the "Server Address" field.
  • Click Connect. Select "Registered User" and enter your PIN.
Quick Command
gio mount "{{ activeWebdavUrl.replace('http://', 'dav://') }}"
  • Open Files and click "Other Locations".
  • Paste the URL above but replace http:// with dav://.
  • Click Connect and enter your PIN if prompted.
