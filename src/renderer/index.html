Connect as Local Drive

1 Quick Command (Tech Method) 

net use Z: "URL"

Paste this into Command Prompt or PowerShell to mount as drive Z:.

2 Step-by-Step (GUI Method)

  • Open File Explorer and click "This PC".
  • In the top menu, click "Map network drive".
  • Paste the URL above into the "Folder" field.
  • Click Finish. If prompted, enter any username and your PIN.

1 Quick Command (Tech Method) 

open "URL"

Paste this into Terminal to mount the drive instantly.

2 Step-by-Step (GUI Method)

  • Open Finder and press Cmd + K.
  • Paste the URL above into the "Server Address" field.
  • Click Connect. Select "Registered User" and enter your PIN.

1 Quick Command (Tech Method) 

gio mount "dav://URL"

Paste this into your terminal (requires gvfs/gio).

2 Step-by-Step (GUI Method)

  • Open Files (Nautilus/Dolphin) and click "Other Locations" or "Network".
  • Paste the URL above but replace http:// with dav://.
  • Click Connect and enter your PIN if prompted.
